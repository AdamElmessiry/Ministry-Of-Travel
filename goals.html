Our Goals

The Ministry of Travel is committed to guiding Egypt toward a brighter future in tourism, culture, and international exchange. Our goals are designed to ensure sustainable development, cultural preservation, and economic prosperity for generations to come.

Tourism Growth

Tourism is one of Egypt’s most vital sectors, and our goal is to steadily increase the number of visitors each year by enhancing infrastructure, improving services, and promoting Egypt’s diverse attractions. We aim to highlight not only ancient monuments but also modern experiences, from luxury resorts on the Red Sea to desert adventures and cultural festivals.

By 2030, the ministry seeks to position Egypt as one of the top five global destinations for cultural and historical tourism.

Cultural Preservation

Egypt’s cultural and archaeological heritage is unmatched, and preserving it is at the heart of our mission. We are committed to protecting ancient monuments, temples, mosques, churches, and traditional crafts through restoration programs, digital documentation, and international collaboration.

Protecting intangible heritage, such as music, folklore, and local traditions, ensures that Egypt’s living culture continues to thrive alongside its monuments.

Sustainability

Sustainable tourism is a core priority. We aim to minimize environmental impacts while maximizing benefits for local communities. This includes supporting eco-friendly hotels, protecting marine life in the Red Sea, and introducing green transportation options for tourists.

Our goal is to ensure that Egypt’s natural and cultural treasures can be enjoyed responsibly by both present and future generations.

International Relations

The Ministry seeks to strengthen Egypt’s position on the global stage through tourism diplomacy. By building partnerships with countries around the world, we aim to increase cultural exchanges, promote Egypt as a safe and welcoming destination, and encourage collaborative research in archaeology and heritage preservation.

Egypt aspires to become a global leader in intercultural dialogue and heritage protection.

Economic Development

Tourism contributes significantly to Egypt’s economy, and our goal is to expand this contribution by diversifying tourist experiences. From luxury travel to budget-friendly adventures, Egypt seeks to attract a wide variety of visitors.

Through job creation, investment in local businesses, and the development of modern facilities, tourism will continue to support Egypt’s economic growth and empower communities across the country.
