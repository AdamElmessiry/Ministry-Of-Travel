Our Services

The Ministry of Travel provides a wide range of services designed to support travelers, businesses, and communities across Egypt. These services are essential for enhancing visitor experiences, promoting sustainable tourism, and strengthening the national economy.

Tourism Services

The Ministry offers resources and guidance for tourists visiting Egypt, including:

  • Information on attractions, heritage sites, and travel routes
  • Guidelines for safe and responsible travel
  • Support for cultural and eco-tourism experiences

Licensing and Regulation

The Ministry regulates and licenses tourism businesses to ensure quality and safety. This includes:

  • Approving hotels, resorts, and tour operators
  • Setting national standards for hospitality and services
  • Monitoring compliance with tourism regulations

Investment Support

The Ministry works with investors and entrepreneurs to expand Egypt’s tourism infrastructure by:

  • Providing investment opportunities and guidance
  • Offering permits and approvals for new tourism projects
  • Encouraging public-private partnerships

Digital Services

Embracing modern technology, the Ministry provides digital solutions such as:

  • Online visa applications and e-visa systems
  • Interactive travel portals and mobile applications
  • Virtual tours of museums and historical sites

Community Support

The Ministry supports local communities by:

  • Promoting small-scale tourism projects
  • Preserving cultural traditions and crafts
  • Creating training programs for tourism workers

International Cooperation

The Ministry builds strong ties with international tourism organizations and governments. Through these partnerships, Egypt enhances global promotion, shares expertise, and welcomes millions of visitors from around the world.
