Leadership

The Ministry of Travel is guided by a team of experienced leaders who are committed to developing Egypt’s tourism sector and ensuring sustainable growth. Their collective expertise shapes the Ministry’s vision, policies, and long-term strategies.

Minister of Travel

The Minister of Travel provides overall direction for Egypt’s tourism sector. The Minister oversees national policies, international cooperation, and the development of strategies that enhance Egypt’s position as a leading global destination.

Deputy Ministers

The Deputy Ministers support the Minister in specialized areas such as tourism development, heritage preservation, investment, and international promotion. Each Deputy Minister supervises specific programs to ensure alignment with the Ministry’s mission and vision.

Directors of Departments

Directors lead the Ministry’s departments and regional offices. They manage areas such as licensing and regulation, research and statistics, marketing campaigns, and community outreach. Their work ensures that the Ministry’s policies are effectively implemented at both the local and national level.

Advisory Council

The Ministry benefits from an Advisory Council composed of experts, academics, and representatives of the private sector. This council provides recommendations on emerging trends, sustainability, and innovation in tourism to keep Egypt’s sector competitive on the global stage.

Commitment to Service

The leadership team is committed to transparency, efficiency, and accountability. By maintaining open communication with citizens, communities, and international partners, the Ministry ensures that its policies reflect the needs of both travelers and Egyptians alike.
